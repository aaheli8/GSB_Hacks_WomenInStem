1 MIT Blossoms BLOSSOMS video lessons provide quality learning experiences in high school classrooms from Brooklyn to Beirut to Bangalore. Over 100 math and science lessons are available to teachers at free of cost . MIT faculty members and partnering educators in Jordan and Pakistan created the first BLOSSOMS lessons, today educators from around the world create and submit BLOSSOMS modules. https://blossoms.mit.edu/videos
2 Coursera Coursera is an American massive open online course provider. It works with universities and other organizations to offer online courses, certifications, and degrees in a variety of subjects. Coursera provides accredited certificates that are recognised by employers. https://www.coursera.org/
3 Udemy Udemy,is an American massive open online course provider aimed at professional adults and students. It provides vast varieties of courses at reasonable rates. The courses on Udemy, once bought, are accessible for lifetime. They do not expire. http://www.udemy.com/
4 edx edX is an American massive open online course provider created by Harvard and MIT. It hosts online university-level courses in a wide range of disciplines to a worldwide student body, including some courses at no charge. Students can enroll with no cost for edX courses in a free audit track, and can receive a professional certificate for reasonable rates http://www.edx.org/
5 W3schools W3Schools is a freemium educational website for learning coding online which provides free courses for beginners. It is an information website, with tutorials and references relating to web development topics such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, SQL,etc.. https://www.w3schools.com/
6 Khan Academy Khan Academy is an American non-profit educational organization created by Sal Khan, with the goal of creating a set of online tools that help educate students. It is an essential homeschooling resource, and provides short lessons in the form of videos along with supplementary practice. It has lessons on math, science, history, grammar, SAT prep, coding, medicine, music, Pixar filmmaking, financial planning, and so much more https://www.khanacademy.org/
